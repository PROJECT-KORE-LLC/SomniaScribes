# SomniaScribes Code of Conduct  
A quiet space deserves quiet care.

SomniaScribes is a warm, local‑only journaling sanctuary created by Project Kore LLC.  
This project welcomes contributions, ideas, and improvements — but always in a way that keeps the space gentle, respectful, and calm.

This Code of Conduct exists to protect that atmosphere.

---

## Our Guiding Principles

### Be Kind  
Treat others with warmth and respect.  
Assume good intentions.  
Speak gently.  
This is a quiet project — keep the tone soft.

### Be Patient  
Everyone moves at their own pace.  
There is no urgency here.  
Take your time, and let others take theirs.

### Be Clear  
Communicate with clarity and calm.  
Offer constructive feedback without pressure or judgment.

### Be Safe  
No harassment, discrimination, hostility, or personal attacks.  
No gatekeeping or belittling.  
No behavior that makes the space feel tense or unsafe.

---

## What We Do Not Allow

- Harassment of any kind  
- Discrimination based on identity or background  
- Aggressive or hostile communication  
- Dismissive or demeaning comments  
- Spam, trolling, or intentionally disruptive behavior  
- Attempts to derail or dominate discussions  
- Sharing harmful, explicit, or abusive content  

If you’re unsure whether something is appropriate, choose the gentler option.

---

## Maintaining the Atmosphere

SomniaScribes is built around four ideas:

- **A simple place to write**
- **Your words stay on your device**  
- **Slow, grounding, atmospheric**  
- **Warm visuals, soft audio, quiet UI**  

Conversations and contributions should honor these principles:  
calm, minimal, private, and warm.

---

## Reporting Concerns

If someone behaves in a way that breaks this atmosphere, please open an issue or contact the maintainers privately at architect.kore@proton.me  
We will respond with care, discretion, and respect.

Project Kore LLC reserves the right to take any action needed to maintain a safe and gentle environment, including:

- clarifying expectations  
- requesting changes  
- moderating discussions  
- removing harmful content  
- limiting or removing participation  

These actions are rare, but the sanctuary comes first.

---

## A Closing Note

Thank you for helping keep SomniaScribes a warm, quiet place.  
Your presence, your tone, and your care shape the experience for everyone who visits this little corner of the internet.

— Project Kore LLC
