# 🌙 SOMNIASCAPES

**Welcome to your cozy digital sanctuary.** 

SomniaScapes is a highly customizable, local-first ambient vibe engine and private thought log. It is designed to turn your device into a true private workshop—a quiet corner of the internet where time is just a suggestion and the cloud isn't invited. 

Snuggle up, brew some tea, and make some magic. ✨

---

## 🔮 The Vibe
We believe your digital environment should be as comforting as your physical one. SomniaScapes combines high-fidelity, expensive-feeling glassmorphism with cozy chaos. It features a live particle engine, dynamic glow effects, and a multi-track audio mixer to help you build the exact atmosphere your brain needs to focus, create, or just exist.

## ✨ Features

* **🎨 Custom Canvases & Vibes:** Choose from 10 stunning color palettes (like *Amethyst*, *Noir*, or *Vellum*) or upload your own local background images to set the scene. 
* **🌧️ Ambient Audio Mixer:** Blend 14 different soundscapes (from *Midnight Owls* to a *Simmering Pot*) or upload your own custom audio files to build the ultimate cozy noise blanket.
* **✨ Sprinkles & Glows:** Control the atmosphere with customizable particle engines (snow, embers, stars) and screen glows. Adjust the speed, density, and opacity to your liking.
* **📓 SomniaScribes:** Your sovereign ambient memory. A beautifully integrated, totally private journal to pour out your thoughts and attach image memories. 
* **💖 The Vault:** Mint your favorite aesthetic configurations to your local vault so you can instantly load your perfect vibe later.
* **📺 Projector Mode:** Beautifully scales to any screen so you can cast the chaos to your TV and transform your entire room.

## 🔒 Privacy First (Seriously.)
There are no servers. There is no cloud. There are no tracking scripts. 

SomniaScapes uses your browser's local `IndexedDB` and `localStorage` to save your custom images, audio, and journal entries directly to your hard drive. Your thoughts and vibes are completely your own. What happens in the vault, stays in the vault. 

## 🚀 How to Run It
Because we like keeping things beautifully simple, there is no terrifying tech setup required. 

1. Download the `somniascapes.html` file (and the `somniascribes.png` default background if you have it!).
2. Double-click the HTML file to open it in any modern web browser.
3. That's literally it. You're in. 

*Pro Tip: Use your browser's "Cast" or "Tab Mirroring" feature to throw SomniaScapes onto your living room TV!*

## 💾 Backing Up Your Memories
Because everything lives happily on your local device, clearing your browser data *will* clear your SomniaScribes journal and saved Vault vibes. 
* Use the **Export Journal** button in the SomniaScribes menu to download your thoughts safely as a JSON file. 
* You can import them back anytime with the **Import Journal** button!
