SOMNIASCRIBES PRO TIPS

Welcome to your highly aesthetic, offline-first digital workshop. Here is how to make the most of it:

  • The Journal: Click the "Journal" button in the top navigation. Your thoughts and attached images are stored safely and permanently inside your device. We use IndexedDB to ensure you don't hit pesky storage limits.
  • Pomodoro Timer: Use the "Timer" tab to execute 25-minute focus sprints. The chime will alert you when it's time to take a break.
  • Vibe Architecture: Mix and match up to 60 total aesthetic variables. Try "Gothic" + "Bats" + "Inferno" for a dark vibe, or "Library" + "Motes" + "Sepia" for deep focus!
  • Cloak UI: Once your vibe is set, hit "Cloak UI" to hide all menus so you can focus entirely on the atmosphere. Click anywhere on the screen to bring the menus back.
  • Data Safety: This app runs entirely on your device (no local!). Make sure you regularly open your Journal and click "Export Journal" to save a backup file to your computer.

🕯️ Pro Tip: The Local Ink Oracle

The Ink Oracle now lives entirely inside the Scriptorium as local lore, room whispers, Nib chatter, and writing atmosphere. No key, account, subscription, or outside service is required. Use Oracle Whisper, Nib & Oracle, and Tower Lore when you want the room to feel alive beside you. Your journal stays local unless you choose to export or share something yourself.
