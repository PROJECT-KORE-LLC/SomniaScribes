PROJECTOR MODE

SomniaScapes runs a live, multi-track rendering engine. To project the full visual and audio experience to a TV, use your browser's native Tab Mirroring.

How to Cast to TV:

1. Right-click anywhere on the background (or open your browser menu).
2. Select "Cast..."
3. Choose your TV/Display from the list.
* Ensure "Cast Tab" is selected in the cast sources menu.
